About Me

I am an Industrial Design student with a strong interest in production-aware design, focusing on packaging, furniture and product systems.

I approach design by balancing concept development with real-world manufacturing constraints, aiming to create solutions that are not only visually refined but also feasible and efficient to produce.

Through hands-on internship experiences, I have gained practical knowledge in technical drawing, materials and production processes.
